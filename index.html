About Us

Our Vision is based on a few core Principles

Assist the elderly to live with dignity until the end

Providing support in order to assist in relieving poverty in and outside of Canada

Assist local charities in their projects

Assist families with children with diabetes to ensure they are physically active

With that in mind U Help Foundation will do its part in ensuring donors and causes are connected in order to improve human existence around the world.

Projects

U-Help Foundation operates on the premise that we can all give back and through helping each other we can all do our part in alleviating poverty and/or improving the quality of life for people and families in and outside of Canada.
